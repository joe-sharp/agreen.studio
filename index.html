About Anita

With over 40 years of experience as a seamstress Anita has seen styles come and go, she has witnessed the brief fads, the fashionable, the anti-fashionable, and the timeless classics. Intent on focusing on styles that weather the test of time, she shares her classic couture collection with you.

Growing up in Saint Paul Minnesota, Anita has a chance to dabble in clothing for all seasons. With the Mall of America right around the corner she is able to connect with clientele from all around the world and see the newest styles from the world's top designers. Frequent trips to the Los Angeles garment district ensure she won't miss anything skipping a midwest presence.

Anita has appeared in the past at the Minneapolis Farmer's Market, dealing in classic fashion clothing and jewelry. She is excited to bring those same styles to a broader audience in the virtual space. She promises to provide the same level of service and care as she continues to embroider pride and love into every piece.

When she isn't creating, Anita enjoys spending time with her husband, three grown-up children and their families. She loves traveling, visiting wineries, and yoga on the beach. During the summer she throws homemade pizza parties baked in a brick oven her husband built in their backyard. Although she loves all of the family and friends in her life, she has a special place in her heart for her son-in-law, he is just such a cool guy.
